About me

My short story

My realization

Since I was a child, I was interested in everything related to computers. I am also a very creative person by nature. When I combined these two factors about myself, I realized that Web development is my true calling. In order to realize my dream of one day being a Web developer, I went to the School of Graphics, Design and Media Production in Zagreb to study Web Design. After the second year, I chose training for a front end developer.

My passion

I am an open-minded person. I love learning new things and broadening my horizons. I am a team player and I love helping others. Years of walking around competitions and gatherings have taught me what it's like to work in a team and help each other. I am very passionate about my job and I really appreciate it. I am a very loyal person when it comes to work and my loved ones.

Why choose me

Like I said before. I am willing to sacrifice myself for my job and learn to improve. I accept criticism very well and take it as an incentive. My knowledge may seem insufficient, but I assure you I am able to convince you otherwise. And if you think that my knowledge is not enough for them, I am ready to go to training and learn new skills as best I can.

My hobbies

As a person who really likes to spend time outside, I do that a lot. Hiking is one of my favorite activities and takes up a lot of my time on weekends. Although sometimes I prefer to go for coffee with friends in the morning to discuss the news in the world and our lives. Another of my extremes is robotics. I love 3D printing parts for simple projects and assembling diodes and chips. Expanding my knowledge about robots and the C#/C++ language is like a small reward.
